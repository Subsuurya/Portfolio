PROFILE PICTURE INSTRUCTIONS
============================

To add your professional picture:

1. Add your professional headshot image to the public folder
2. Name it exactly: "profile-picture.jpg"
3. Recommended size: 400x400 pixels or larger (square aspect ratio)
4. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, or WebP

The image will automatically appear in the Hero section.
If no image is found, it will show your initials "JD" as a fallback.

Current fallback initials: "JD"
To change the initials, edit the Hero.jsx component and replace "JD" with your initials.
